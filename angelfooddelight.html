Angel Food Delight

Category
Dessert
Prep Time
20 min
Cook Time
No-bake
Servings
10-12

Recipe Story

Remembering recipes wasn't easy but this recipe was one of the first to come to mind. It was definitely a staple in our house for a good stretch of the nineties. It was served in a large glass bowl, perhaps a trifle bowl.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 oz) angel food cake, pinched or torn into very small pieces
  • 8 Butterfinger bars, crushed very fine
  • 1 stick butter, melted
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 (16 oz) Cool Whip

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix melted butter, Cool Whip, and powdered sugar together until combined.
  2. Tear the angel food cake into very small pieces.
  3. Crush the Butterfinger candy very fine.
  4. In a serving dish, layer cake pieces first.
  5. Add a layer of the Cool Whip mixture.
  6. Add a layer of crushed candy.
  7. Repeat layers until ingredients are used, ending with a Cool Whip layer and candy on top.
  8. Chill before serving for best texture.

Recipe Summary

I found this recipe in Grandma's recipe box. It was handwritten in her hand writing. Though I can't find the recipe there was also a variation that used Heath Bars instead of Butterfinger. They were both very good.
