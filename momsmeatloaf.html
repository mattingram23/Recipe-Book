Recipe Story

Linda's classic meat loaf is a family staple, baked in two loaves and topped with bacon and ketchup.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds ground beef
  • 3 whole eggs
  • 2 1/2 cups cracker crumbs
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons Accent
  • 2/3 cup warm water
  • 1 package onion soup mix
  • Bacon strips for topping
  • Extra ketchup for topping

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mix all ingredients together.
  3. Shape into two loaves and place in loaf pans.
  4. Top each loaf with bacon strips, then cover with ketchup.
  5. Bake for 1 hour.

Recipe Summary

This recipe makes two loaves, making it great for larger family meals.
