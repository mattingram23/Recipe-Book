Servings
10-12
Prep Time
25 min
Cook Time
10 min
Difficulty
Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 stick butter
  • 1 1/4 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup sugar
  • Cream cheese, cool whip, and topping of choice

Instructions

  1. 1Mix crust ingredients until crumbly and bake until golden.
  2. 2Reserve one-third of crust for topping, then cool completely.
  3. 3Whip filling ingredients, spread over crust, and add fruit topping.
  4. 4Finish with reserved crumble and chill before serving.
