Recipe Story

A sweet holiday pie from Linda with a classic pecan filling and simple pantry ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white corn syrup
  • 1 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1/3 teaspoon salt
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 3 whole eggs, slightly beaten
  • 1 heaping cup pecans
  • 1 unbaked 9-inch pie shell

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mix corn syrup, brown sugar, salt, melted butter, and vanilla.
  3. Add slightly beaten eggs and stir until combined.
  4. Pour into an unbaked 9-inch pie shell and sprinkle pecans over filling.
  5. Bake about 45 minutes.

Recipe Summary

Let the pie cool before slicing so the filling sets cleanly.
