Recipe Story

These roll-out sugar cookies from Linda are great for holidays and decorating with family.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups powder sugar
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 whole egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/2 teaspoon almond flavoring
  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • Extra sugar for sprinkling (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix sugar and butter, then add egg and flavorings.
  2. Blend well.
  3. Sift dry ingredients with flour, then blend into the butter mixture.
  4. Refrigerate dough 2 to 3 hours.
  5. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  6. Roll dough to about 1/16 inch thickness, then cut shapes and sprinkle with sugar if desired.
  7. Bake 7 to 8 minutes or until delicately brown.
  8. Cool completely before adding icing or decorations.

Recipe Summary

Chilled dough is easier to roll and helps cookies hold their shape.
