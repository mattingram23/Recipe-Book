Recipe Story

This classic cheese ball from Linda is a family favorite for gatherings, especially around the holidays.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) cream cheese
  • 1/2 package shredded cheese
  • 1 package Buddig beef, cut into small squares (reserve half for coating)
  • 2 teaspoons mustard
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 cup onion or green onions, diced
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients together, using only half of the cut-up beef in the mixture.
  2. Shape mixture into a ball.
  3. Roll the cheese ball in the reserved cut-up beef.
  4. Optional: make finger foods by spreading about 2 teaspoons of mixture on halved beef slices and rolling into logs.

Recipe Summary

Serve chilled with crackers, pretzels, or fresh vegetables.
