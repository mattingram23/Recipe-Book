Recipe Story

This comforting skillet-and-bake dish combines seasoned beef, tomatoes, pasta, and melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 3 cans diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups uncooked macaroni
  • Salted water for boiling macaroni
  • Sliced cheese for topping

Instructions

  1. Brown the ground beef in a skillet.
  2. Add diced tomatoes and continue cooking on medium heat for 5 to 10 minutes to reduce liquid.
  3. Cook macaroni in boiling salted water for 7 minutes.
  4. Add cooked macaroni to the beef and tomato mixture in the skillet, then cook until macaroni is tender.
  5. Top with slices of cheese and bake at 350 degrees until cheese is melted.

Recipe Summary

A hearty one-pan style meal that reheats well the next day.
