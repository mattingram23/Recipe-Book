Apple Crumb Pie

Recipe Story

A cozy apple pie with warm spices and a buttery crumb topping.

Ingredients

Pie Filling

  • 1 (9-inch) pie shell
  • 6 cups thinly sliced peeled apples
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup apple butter (optional)

Crumb Topping

  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 5 tablespoons butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. In a large bowl, toss apples with lemon juice.
  3. Mix sugar, flour, cinnamon, and nutmeg; sprinkle over apples and stir. Stir in apple butter if using.
  4. Spoon filling into pie shell.
  5. Prepare crumb topping by mixing flour, brown sugar, and cinnamon. Cut in butter until crumbly.
  6. Sprinkle topping over apples.
  7. Cover loosely with foil and bake 25 minutes.
  8. Remove foil and bake 25 to 30 minutes more, until apples are tender when tested with a toothpick.

Recipe Summary

If the topping browns early, tent loosely with foil for the final bake time.
