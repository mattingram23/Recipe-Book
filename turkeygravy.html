Recipe Story

Linda's gravy card includes both turkey gravy and a quick chicken or sausage gravy variation.

Ingredients

Turkey Gravy

  • 2 cups turkey broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch

Chicken or Sausage Gravy

  • 2 to 3 tablespoons pan drippings (oil)
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Turkey gravy: Bring broth to a boil.
  2. Mix cornstarch with water until smooth, then add to pan.
  3. Stir constantly, bring back to a boil, and cook 1 minute until thickened. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Chicken or sausage gravy: After browning meat, leave 2 to 3 tablespoons fat in skillet.
  5. Whisk flour into fat and cook 1 minute.
  6. While whisking, pour in milk and bring to a boil.
  7. Lower heat and simmer gently for 2 minutes.
  8. Season to taste with salt and pepper.

Recipe Summary

Both versions come together quickly and are best served hot.
