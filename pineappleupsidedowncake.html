Pineapple Upside Down Cake

Recipe Story

A nostalgic pineapple upside down cake with cherries and a buttery brown sugar topping.

Ingredients

  • 1 can sliced pineapple
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • Maraschino cherries
  • 1/3 cup shortening
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 whole egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 1/4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup pineapple juice (add water if needed)
  • 1 tablespoon pineapple syrup

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Melt butter, then add brown sugar and 1 tablespoon pineapple syrup. Arrange pineapples in skillet and add cherries.
  3. Cream shortening and sugar. Add egg, vanilla, dry ingredients, and 1/2 cup juice.
  4. Pour batter over pineapple layer and bake 40 to 45 minutes.
  5. Cool 5 minutes, then invert onto a plate.

Recipe Summary

For best release, invert while still warm after a short cooling period.
