Recipe Story

A comforting, old-fashioned chicken and dumplings recipe with homemade dumplings.

Ingredients

Broth

  • 1 pound boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 medium carrots, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon sage
  • 1 (48 ounce) can chicken broth

Dumplings

  • 2 cups flour
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 tablespoons oil
  • 1 cup milk
  • Extra flour as needed for handling dough
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup mixed with 1/2 cup milk

Instructions

  1. Add all broth ingredients together and simmer until chicken is tender. Shred the chicken.
  2. Mix dumpling ingredients. Roll dough on a floured board, adding flour until it is easy to handle. Cut into squares or strips.
  3. Optional: make dumplings the day before so they dry slightly and are easier to handle.
  4. Bring broth to a gentle boil. Add dumplings a few at a time, gently pulling dumplings back in the pot so you can add more.
  5. Add cream of chicken soup mixed with 1/2 cup milk and simmer 10 to 15 minutes.

Recipe Summary

This recipe is especially good for cool weather and family dinners.
