Dorothy's Punch

Category
Drink
Prep Time
10 min
Cook Time
No-cook
Servings
Party size

Recipe Story

A family party favorite, Dorothy’s Punch is fruity, bright, and made ahead so it’s ready when everyone arrives. This is the original recipe card version.

Ingredients

  • 2 Packages Cherry Kool-Aid
  • 1 Package Orange Kool-Aid
  • 1 1/2 Quarts Water
  • 3 Cups Sugar
  • 1 Large Can Pineapple Juice
  • 3 Large Bananas, blended
  • 3 Small 7Ups
  • 3 Tablespoons Lemon Juice
  • 1 Box Frozen Strawberries, mashed
  • 1 or 2 Oranges, cut in half and sliced with peeling on

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients together.
  2. Freeze the punch mixture and stir several times while it is freezing so everything blends evenly.
  3. Remove from the freezer several hours before serving.

Recipe Summary

This recipe makes a large party batch and is best prepared ahead. Stirring during freezing helps keep the fruit and juices well combined.
